Greetings from New York City!

I am Forest White, a full stack developer and quantitative researcher from silicon valley. Today, I live and work in New York City. I've worked for several ecommerce and software development firms, including eBay, CNET, BEA, HP, Actuate, and Google. I conduct my research in cooperation with Christopher C. Gerry and his recent Ph.D. graduate Richard Birrittella.

The bulk of my research concerns quantum optics. I've developed my own multithreaded Java application specifically to perform large sum calculations required for quantum statistics in some complex systems, such as two-mode cavity Quantum Electrodynamics.
